Emrul Hasan

Hello! I'm Emrul Hasan, currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Computer Science at Toronto Metropolitan University (TMU) in Toronto, Canada. My research focuses on multicriteria recommendation system and explainability. I apply advanced NLP and Deep Learning techniques to extract features from unstructured text, leveraging them to learn users fine-grained preferences and enhancing the explainability of the recommendation system. I have over 5 years of experiences in implementing Machine Learning, Deep Learning, NLP to various real-world problems including recommendation systems, Fake News detection, Summarization, Information Retrieval, and Sentiment Analysis. Currently I am working as a machine learning technical specialist at Vector Institute, Toronto, Canada where I provide technial supports (e.g. prompt tuning and fine-tuning llm for document retrieval tasks) to machine learning associates. In the summar 2024, I worked at Vector Institute as an Applied Mahcine Leanring Intern. As an itern I was involved in NewsMediaBias-Plus dataset development. I particularly focused on multimodal news article annotation, and fine-tuning multimodal models for benchmark development. For text annotation, we have applied LLama series (llama2, llama3, and llama3.1) wheras for image annotation we have used CLIP, LLaVA series, miniCPM, Phi3 Vision, and PaliGemma etc. My expertise in Machine Learning frameworks includes Python, PyTorch, Scikit-Learn, Pandas, Numpy, SQL, NLTK, SpaCy, LangGraph, Huggingface, SLURM, CUDA, etc. Prior to joining at TMU, I obtained my MS in Physics and Astronomy from the University of Manitoba in Winnipeg, MB, Canada.

I also have 5+ years of experience with Teaching Assistance in various undergraduate and graduate courses including database management, programming (e.g., Python, C, and Matlab), data analytics, data mining, natural language processing, and data warehousing. I am very passionate about AI and innovation, and a quick learner.
