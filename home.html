Hello! I’m Emrul Hasan, a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Toronto Metropolitan University. My research focus lies in the interaction of NLP, Multimodal LLM, Reponsible AI, Recommendation Systems and Explainability. I have applied advanced NLP and Deep Learning techniques to enhance user preference understanding and recommendation transparency. With over 5 years of experience in Machine Learning and NLP, I’ve solved various real-world challenges like recommendation systems, news media bais detection, document retrieval, personalization, and sentiment analysis.

Currently, I work as a Machine Learning Technical Specialist at Vector Institute, providing guidence on RAG-agent development for automating the workflow. Previously, as an Applied Machine Learning Intern, I contributed to the NewsMediaBias-Plus project, focusing on multimodal news article dataset creation and basline development. We fine-tuned LLMs and VLLMs for benchmark model development. I’m proficient in Python, PyTorch, LangGraph, Scikit-Learn, SLURM, and various NLP tools.

Before TMU, I earned my MS in Physics and Astronomy from the University of Manitoba. I also have 5+ years of teaching experience in courses like database management and data analytics. I’m passionate about AI and a quick learner. I am looking for an opportunities in Recommendation System, Multimodal LLMs, Generative AI, and AI for social Good.
