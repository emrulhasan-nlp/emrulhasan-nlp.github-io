Emrul Hasan

Hello! I'm Emrul Hasan, currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Computer Science at Toronto Metropolitan University in Toronto, ON, Canada. I obtained my MS in Physics and Astronomy from the University of Manitoba in Winnipeg, MB, Canada. My research delves into Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Natural Language Processing, with a specific focus on Recommendation Systems. I have over 5 years of experiences in implementing Machine Learning, Deep Learning, NLP, and LLMs to various real-world problems including recommendation systems, Fake News classification, Summarization, Information Retrieval, and Sentiment Analysis. My expertise in Data Science/AI technologies includes Python, PyTorch, Scikit-Learn, Pandas, Numpy, SQL, NLTK, SpaCy, Huggingface, CUDA, etc.

I also have 5+ years of experience with Teaching Assistance in various undergraduate and graduate courses including database management, programming (e.g., Python, C, and Matlab), data analytics, data mining, natural language processing, and data warehousing. I am very passionate about AI and innovation, and a quick learner.
